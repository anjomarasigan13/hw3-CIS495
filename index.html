About Bootstrap Fitness

Bootstrap Fitness is the leading, all encompassing gym in St. Louis.

Explore the future of strength and conditioning with a lineup of the industry best personal trainers. Discover new techniques to advance your fitness level whether you're a beginner or a veteran.

We cater to all shapes and sizes from all walks of life. We address weight-loss, sports performance training, body building/appearance training, and rehabilitation.

Expert Trainers

Our expert trainers are experienced, educated, and hold multiple industry standard certifications: C-PT, ATC, CSCS, ACSM, Certified Dietician, Functional Pattern Certified, Postural Restoration Certified, TRX Certified, AR Level 1-3 Certified, and Kettlebell Certified.

Bootstrap Fitness Trainers are committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everybody. They look forward to educating, energizing, and empowering clients each and everyday.

Services we offer:

  • One-on-One Personal Training
  • Semi Private Training
  • Group Fitness
  • Speed & Agility Training
  • Plyometrics Training
  • Massage Therapy
  • Physical Therapy
  • Pre-Op/Post-Op Rehabilitation
  • Nutrition Consultation
